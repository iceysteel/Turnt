Think Kickstarter but for parties

When you wanna throw a party just add where you're gonna have it and set your goal for how much money and party supplies you need.

Invite Friends

Invite all your buddies to turn up and help get supplies and money from them. Don't worry, only your friends can see your party, and it will be deleted after it happens!

Turn UP

Once enough people pledge to your party to meet your goals, the party is good to go! Just have a great time!
